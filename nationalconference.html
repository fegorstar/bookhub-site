About the Conference

Reclaiming Academic Sovereignty: Nigeria’s Stakeholder Conference on Open Access Book Publishing

Reclaiming Academic Sovereignty is a national gathering dedicated to a coordinated, community‑led framework for Open Access (OA) book publishing in Nigeria. Under the theme “From Pilots to Policy,” the conference unites authors, editors, librarians, university leaders, funders, and policymakers to shape the next phase of Nigeria’s OA journey.

Expect inspiring keynotes, platform demos, and collaborative policy sprints aimed at national consensus on Diamond Open Access — removing both author and reader fees while boosting local ownership and global reach.

This is a watershed moment—moving from successful pilots to a shared strategy that empowers scholars, strengthens infrastructure, and opens research to the world.
